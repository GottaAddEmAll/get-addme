About

AddMe is a mobile application that allows you to add and organize your contacts among different social media platforms. By scanning another user's QR code, AddMe allows you to add them to your contacts, email, Snapchat, Facebook, and Twitter.

It erases the need to have to go through the painstaking process of adding people individually to each of these accounts, making connecting with others less awkward and more convenient. Whether you're a student looking for an easier way to add people, or a professional who wants to better manage contacts, this app is the best way to start! Download now for iPhone or Android.
